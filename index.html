Our Story

Who we are

For over ten years, we have been serving the Tumwater community here on Trosper Road. We believe that every person and family deserves a nice and delicious meal. That is why we take great strides into making your meal simply delightful.

With each meal you order from us, we promise friendly service and delicious food. Whether you want to take out or dine in, come and enjoy our food.
